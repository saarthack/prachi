Creator of India's National Flag

Pingali Venkayya was an Indian freedom fighter. He was a staunch follower of Mahatma Gandhi and the designer of the flag on which the Indian national flag was based. He was born in Telugu brahmin familyat Bhatlapenumarru, near Machilipatnam, in what is now the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh

BLUE

Blue colour of the Ashok Chakra in our Flag denotes the Continous Progress of the Country. The Blue in our flag signifies the boundless sky above and the fathomless sea below. The Blue also implies inner energy. The Chakra with its 24 spokes suggests the continual progress of the country ..

GREEN

In the national flag of India the top band is of Saffron colour, indicating the strength and courage of the country. The white middle band indicates peace and truth with Dharma Chakra. The last band is green in colour shows the fertility, growth and auspiciousness of the land..
